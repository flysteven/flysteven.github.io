Keynote Speakers

Prof. Steven M. LaValle

Steven M. Lavalle is a Professor, Angel Investor and Corporate Advisor at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. His recent research interest has been on artificial intelligence. Academically, he is best known for his book Planning Algorithms, one of the most highly cited texts in the field.

Since September 2012, he started helping Oculus VR with head tracking when it was only a few people. In March 2013, he joined full-time in Irvine as the head scientist, mainly working on tracking and perceptual psychology. He was the Principal Scientist of Oculus Rift. In March 2014, Oculus VR was purchased by Facebook.

Prof. Lavalle returned to the university in August 2014. Starting in Spring 2015, he offers a UIUC course on virtual reality. Based on the course, he has been making progress on a new book, Virtual Reality. His recent work was also highlighted in his interview with Chicago Tribune.

To learn more about Prof. Steven M. LaValle, check his website HERE.

Prof. Weng Cho Chew

Weng Cho Chew is George and Ann Fisher Distinguished Professor of Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and a member of the US National Academy of Engineering. Previously, he served as the Dean of Faculty of Engineering at the University of Hong Kong, and the first Y.T. Lo Endowed Chair Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois. Academically, Prof. Chew was widely known for his work in Electromagnetics and Optics. He is a Fellow of IEEE, OSA, IOP, Electromagnetics Academy, Hong Kong Institute of Engineers, and was an NSF Presidential Young Investigator.

To learn more about Prof. Weng Cho Chew, check his website HERE.

Mr. Pubin Liang

Pubin Liang is Managing Director of Hainan Airlines North America. Joined Hainan Airlines since 1996, Pubin has had worked in different positions within the airlines which equipped him with both operational and commercial experience. He was transferred to US to lead Hainan airlines US team since 2012. As one of subsidiaries of HNA Group, Hainan Airlines has a fleet of over 150 aircrafts serving more than 90 cities in domestic China and 30 destinations worldwide. Hainan Airlines’ North America network includes 8 routes connecting Beijing, Shanghai, Changsha to Seattle, San Jose, Los Angeles, Chicago, Boston, Toronto and Calgary in coming June.

Ms. Xiaoming Li

Xiaoming Li currently serves as the Associate Engineering Advisor and formerly as the Principal Research Scientist at Eli Lilly and Company. Dr. Li has over 25 year broad experiences in the Pharmaceutical Industry. Dr. Li is also the chair of Lilly Chinese Culture Network under Lilly Global Diversity and Inclusion Office with over 800 members. Dr. Li holds a Ph.D. in Environmental-Chemical Engineering from California Institute of Technology and a B.S. in Environmental Chemistry from Peking University.

Mr. Eugene Zhang

Eugene Zhang is the founding partner and managing director of TEEC (Tsinghua Entrepreneur & Executive Club) Angel Fund. Prior to TEEC Angel Fund, he was the founder and CEO of Innospring and JEDA Technologies, Inc. Mr. Zhang holds his M.S.C.E from Syracuse University, and a M.S.E.E and B.S.E.E from Tsinghua University, Beijing.

Laura Frerichs

Laura Frerichs is the University of Illinois Research Park Director and Director of Economic Development for the University of Illinois’ Urbana-Champaign campus. She is responsible for managing startup company services at the EnterpriseWorks technology incubator, marketing the Research Park, and supporting the University’s economic development efforts.

Speakers

Vincent He

Vincent He currently serves as the Director of Risk Management in Discover Financial Services. He is a business result driven and intuitive senior leader in advanced analytics for all consumer lending areas. Mr. He has well rounded business experience in general management, resource allocation and complex project management with polished executive presentation skill and enthusiastic personality along with 16 years of analytics and business experience. Mr. He has a MBA degree from Kellogg School of Management in Northwestern University.

Yi (Elaine) Li

Elaine Li is the founder and CEO of Zonvon. She has worked for Morgan Stanley Investment Management, AllianceBernstein, the investment division of The Ford Foundation, and the asset allocation division of Prudential Financial. Elaine has done research on Pattern Recognition and Artificial Intelligence at Tsinghua University, Chinese Academy of Sciences and NEC China. She has been invited as final judge of premiere startup competitions by Cheung Kong Graduate School of Business, Tencent, Columbia University, etc. Elaine serves as mentor of ZiJing Houde Investor Business School, board member of portfolio companies and advisor of startups.

Chen Li

Chen Li is the CMO & Co-Founder of Liaoyuan Startup Social Networking platform marketing specialist with ample experience of working in business developing and diversified marketing. After graduating from Central University of Finance and Economics, Chen pursued his master degree on Political Psychology at Stony Brook University. Prior to his experience at Liaoyuan, Chen has worked as a project manager in a NGO, conducted and coordinated several large campaigns including a New Year countdown event at Time Square and promotion of NBA basketball games.

Yansong Jiang

Yansong Jiang currently serves as the VP at JPMorgan Chase. He was also the founding partner of iBeeHub, a startup incubator located in New York. Yansong specialized in database marketing, Internet finance and credit risk management. He had many years of working experiences in top finance companies including Citigroup and FICO. He holds a PhD degree in Marketing Communication from University of Connecticut and a BS degree in Physics from Peking University.
